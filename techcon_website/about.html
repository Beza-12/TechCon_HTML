Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers passionate about open source technologies. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most respected tech conferences globally, featuring thousands of attendees from over 50 countries.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to empower creators and foster innovation in the technology community. Our mission is to provide a platform for learning, collaboration, and inspiration for developers, designers, engineers, and entrepreneurs.

Notable Past Speakers
